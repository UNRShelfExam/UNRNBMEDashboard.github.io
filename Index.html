Welcome to the UNR Med NBME Shelf Exam Self Scoring Dashboard. Use this tool to determine
your percentile for each Shelf Exam and to compare your progress across exam sub-categories.

To check your precentile, select the tab for the exam, select the quarter that you took
the exam in, enter your EPC, and click Show my Score. This will plot your percentile
on the chart and also display your score underneath the textbox.

To track your progress in a sub-catecory across exams, first click on the Comparative Data
tab. Within this tab you can manually enter the sub-category you are tracking as well as how
well you did compared to the average in the 7 exams. Select either Higher, Lower, or Same for
however many exams had this sub-category. These oints are then plotted on the chart to give you an
idea on your standing within each sub-category.

Note that you can return to this screen at any time by clicking on the webpage's title banner.
